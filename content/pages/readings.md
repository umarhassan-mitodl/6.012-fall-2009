-------------

MOS = metal-on-silicon  
MOSFET = metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistor  
BJT = bipolar junction transistor  
CMOS = complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor  
CS = common source  
OCTC = open circuit time constant
